# Governance

Fulgurite is maintained as a source-available project with public collaboration, centralized product direction and reserved commercial rights.

## Project Model

Maintainers are responsible for:

- product roadmap decisions
- release management
- security policy
- repository moderation
- architectural direction
- licensing and commercial policy
- accepting, declining or reshaping contributions

The current functional roadmap is described in [ROADMAP.md](ROADMAP.md).

## Decision-Making

Maintainers may accept, reject, defer or request changes to contributions based on:

- product fit
- security impact
- maintainability
- architecture consistency
- legal clarity
- support burden
- documentation quality

Major changes should be discussed before implementation.

## Contributor Rights

Fulgurite follows a contributor-first model:

- contributors keep ownership of original material they submit
- contributors remain responsible for the origin and legality of their submissions
- contributors grant the project the rights described in [CONTRIBUTOR_TERMS.md](CONTRIBUTOR_TERMS.md)
- contributor-authored features stay in the public source-available track unless the contributor agrees otherwise in writing

## Commercial Boundaries

The project owner may build commercial offerings around Fulgurite, including paid support, hosted services, commercial licensing, premium integrations or future modules.

Maintainers will not move a contributor-authored feature into a paid-only, commercial-only, hosted premium or paid API feature without prior written agreement from that contributor.

## Documentation And Language

English root documents are authoritative for the repository.

French convenience versions live in [project/fr](project/fr/README.md). User documentation lives in [docs/en](docs/en/01-index.md) and [docs/fr](docs/fr/01-index.md).

## Conflict Handling

When ownership, licensing, roadmap or product-boundary questions are unclear, maintainers may pause, narrow or decline a contribution until the issue is clarified in writing.
